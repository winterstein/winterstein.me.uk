In Memory: Joe Ruddy




It is with great sadness that I write this. My brother-in-law Joe Ruddy died last Sunday. He was just 31. Joe was a musician, artist, and a natural comic. Even during his illness he always kept his positive attitude and sense of humour. Most of all he was a lovely good-natured man and the perfect husband. It is a terribly cruel fate that he should die so young and just a couple of months before his wife is due to give birth. I'm sure he would have been a wonderful father.

Donations to Cancer Research can be made in memory of Joe.
