The Sharp End of The Bullet




Scene 5: You Just Can't Trust Some Criminal Lunatics

[MUSCLES and HARRY enter] MUSCLES: Guess what Smartshit? EDDY: If you boys are selling raffle tickets, I'm afraid the girl guides already cleaned me out. HARRY: No, there's been another killing. And again the victim was of Jewish persuasion. EDDY: And now it's too late to persuade him otherwise. Another note? HARRY: Sort of. This time they wrote it on the alley wall, using the victim's own blood. EDDY: The third letter of the name, right? HARRY: No. It said: 'The last letter of The Name has been uttered.' EDDY: The last? HARRY: But this one wasn't working for me. EDDY: Who was he? HARRY: Some Ginsberg character. No-one I ever heard of. EDDY: [perplexed] It's not right. HARRY: Maybe, but it seems this concludes it. Here's your money. Less 500 for my protection of course. EDDY: You're dropping the case? We can't quit this case. Three men are dead. HARRY: What of it? There are many left. EDDY: Two of your own men, and you'll let the murderers get away? HARRY: As long as it's a religious thing, who are we to interfere? You're talking about imposing western values on another culture. EDDY: Jews are western Harry. HARRY: Even so, I wouldn't want that shit on my conscience. EDDY: Your conscience Harry? Your conscience is hiding out in a warm whorehouse someplace without an extradition treaty. HARRY: Don't get too smart Eddy. Your protection money will only go so far. I'm a businessman, and this cabala shit ain't my business. [makes to depart] You coming Muscles? MUSCLES: I'm going to talk a little with Smartshit here if that's OK Boss. HARRY: Sure. [exit HARRY] EDDY: [produces a hip flask & pours two shots] To liver failure. [they drink] EDDY: Something's not right Muscles. I think there's going to be another murder. MUSCLES: It's written here. This was the last. EDDY: Maybe it's written different somewhere else. You can't go trusting notes written by criminals, especially insane ones. Remember, the name of god has 4 letters in Jewish tradition. And the murders were committed on the 4th, the 8th and the 12th of the month. All multiples of 4. They're symmetrical in time. MUSCLES: So there's another one coming in four days. EDDY: Right. Symmetrical in time... Find me a map of the city. [MUSCLES looks around blankly] In the bookshelf [EDDY gestures, MUSCLES procures a map]. The first murder took place in the Windsor Hotel. [he marks the map] The second was here. [another mark] Where was the latest murder? MUSCLES: Corner of Lincoln and Janus. [EDDY plots the third point] MUSCLES: Well look at that. EDDY: Three corners of a perfect square. Which means... MUSCLES: The 4th murder will be here. EDDY: Symmetrical in time and space. MUSCLES: What are you going to do? EDDY: [produces a gun] Get there early and bust the party. [pockets the gun again] In the meantime... [pours another two shots] MUSCLES: To drunkeness and beyond. [they drink] You know for a guy who collaborates with cops, you ain't too bad. EDDY: For a brutal thug, neither are you.
