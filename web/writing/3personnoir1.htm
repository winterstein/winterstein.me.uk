The Sharp End of The Bullet




Scene 1: An Aggressive Recruitment Policy

[A large man enters, draws a gun and points it at EDDY's head] EDDY: Shit, are my library books overdue already? MUSCLES: No Smartshit, you're subscription to Amnesty ran out. They want to know why you don't care no more. EDDY: I no longer believe in innocent civilians. MUSCLES: That just breaks my heart Smartshit. But now that we got the pleasantries out the way, you're coming with me. The Boss has a job for you. EDDY: Not me. I've got too much on already. MUSCLES: Perhaps you don't know who I work for. EDDY: Muscles Mallone, you work homicide for Sweetface Harry. MUSCLES: That's right and he has a job for you. EDDY: Tell Harry sorry, but I've got too much on already. MUSCLES: Maybe you should tell him yourself. [cocks gun] EDDY: You're not going to use that. MUSCLES: You certain? It ain't wise to be too certain. [silence] MUSCLES: Well maybe. [lowers gun] But I also got this. [holds up a fist] Any second now, your dental records could be real out of date. EDDY: You've got a way with words. Anyone ever tell you that? MUSCLES: Just because I look stupid don't mean I am. EDDY: It's coincidental then. MUSCLES: I also look like a man who could beat you into a sticky pulp roughly the colour and consistency of raspberry jam without breaking sweat. EDDY: Careful; I stain.
