recipes Ingredients
bicarbonate of soda: 2 tsp
cream of tartar: 2 tsp
wholemeal flour: 225 grams / ½ lb / 8 oz / 2 cups
plain floor: 225 grams / ½ lb / 8 0z / 2 cups
sugar: 1 tsp
salt: 1 tsp
butter: 40 grams / 1 ½ oz / 3 tbsp
buttermilk: approx 300 ml / 10 fl.oz / 1 ¼ cups
  1. preheat the oven to gas mark 5
  2. sift the flour, soda, tartar, salt & sugar together into a large bowl
  3. rub in the butter
  4. mix in sufficient buttermilk to make a soft dough
  5. slightly flour a surface and shape into a circle slightly less than an inch thick
  6. using a sharp knife, mark a cross across the circle
  7. sprinkle with flour
  8. place on a greased baking tray
  9. bake for 35 minutes
No kneading, no leaving to rise
