Recipes

Paste:

600g ground poppy seed
300g sugar
lemmon juice, milk

Cook for 5 minutes.

Pastry:

8oz (1 2/3 cups) self-raising flour
5oz (145g) butter
4oz (2/3 cup) soft brown sugar
2oz (1/3 cup) ground almonds
1 egg

Mix the dry ingredients. Rub in the butter. Add the egg and make into a dough. Fridge for 30 min / 1 hour. Roll half, add filling, roll other half. sprinkle with demerara sugar, add almonds cook 35 minutes medium hot.
