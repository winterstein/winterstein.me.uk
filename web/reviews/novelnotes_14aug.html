Jerome K Jerome's Novel Notes - Beset (page 91)
Drams 3
Venue Greyfriars Kirk House (28)
Address 86 Candlemaker Row, 225 6575

Novel Notes by Jerome K Jerome, author of Three Men in a Boat, is a light-hearted comedy on himself and his society. On a dreary afternoon, a few friends who set about writing a first rate novel, but get distracted telling countless short anecdotes.

The cast of four play parts from beetles to victorian gentlemen as they act out the different tales. They're energetic and enjoy themselves. It's a bit like watching children's TV as they dress up and ham it up.

Sadly the show doesn't quite work. The 19th century characters are inevitably outdated, and the production is too jolly to be funny. A shame; with a drier delivery a couple of the stories could be acidly funny. Nonetheless Novel Notes is a happy amusing show.

Runs 14-18,21-25,27 Aug at 12:35pm (1:35). Tickets £6 (£5)
