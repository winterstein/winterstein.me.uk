Stills Gallery
Location - 23 Cockburn Street
Opening times - Tues-Sat 10am-5pm
Admission - Free
Website - www.stills.org, currently not working
current exhibition    permanent collection

This small but airy gallery specialises in contemporary photography and video art, but also showcases sculpture, installations and other work. Styles and subjects are varied, as is the quality. An excellent cafe / restaurant overlooks the gallery.
