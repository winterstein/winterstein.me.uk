Disappeared - Beg, Borrow & Steal (page 61)
Drams 2 and a half
Venue The Underbelly (61)
Address Edinburgh Central Library, George IV bridge (entrance via Cowgate), 620 6206
A young woman meets a strange man in a bar and is never seen again. Did he kill her? Or did she just... vanish?

Disappeared presents The City (you know, the big anonymous one) as a hell where everyone is trapped and everyone is alone. It views modern city life as so bleak that completely disconnecting from friends, family and possibly life itself can be seen as a triumph.

Unfortunately Phyllis Nagy's script is heavy handed, and only the two leads have depth. Beg, Borrow & Steal have done a competent production though.
Runs 5-11 Aug at 5pm (6:45). Tickets £6.50 (£5.50)
