Murdoch Clinches TV Deal for the Revolution.
The Leader ended weeks of speculation by announcing his approval for the Murdoch bid. The revolution will be shown on a 'Pay-Per-Coup' system. See BSkyB listings for details.

Monopolies & Mergers Commission to Investigate The Leader.
The investigation, announced this morning, will focus on the well known claim that "There is only one will & that is the will of the people expressed through The Leader". If the commission finds against The Leader, he will have to be broken up into several 'baby leaders'. In a similar move on the other side of the Atlantic, the US Government has brought an anti-trust suit against The Leader on the grounds that he was insisting retailers package his ideology with his revolutionary products, thus denying market share to smaller ideologies which could have benefited the consumer.

Revolution Announces Record Profits.
The Revolution today announced profits for this quarter that set a new world record. A spokesman at the A.G.M. attributed the spectacular performance of the movement to the continued success of the "expropriation-of-the-land-and-property-of-the-robber---baron-capitalists-and-their-lackey-running-dogs" policy. Industry experts estimate this scheme to have thus far encompassed all wealth everywhere.
