INTERNATIONAL ROUND UP

MEXICO

Stirring news from the deprived regions of Zapatas and Fajitas. In a spontaneous display of support for the party, the peasants now grow only red chillies. Not only do the fields of red assert the dignity and solidarity of the working man, but their extra heat will burn the mouths and buttocks of the capitalist oppressor.
   - Hernando Gonzales, Secretary of the Mexican Party

ANTARCTICA

Brothers, we must call upon your aid! Do not sit back and allow the black man to continue to be subjugated here. Antarctica - 'the white continent' - is destined to remain so now that the corrupt western powers, frightened by the potency of the black man, have signed the Antarctic Preservation Treaty. A treaty openly designed to crush the rights and the spirit of this wildernesses' working black lower class. Already our membership has plummeted to nothing. We call on our penguin brothers to throw off the outdated titles of Emperor and King and become Proletariat Penguins!
   - Pygoscelis adeliae, Secretary of the Antarctic Party
